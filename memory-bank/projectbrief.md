# REKT Memecoin Website - Project Brief

## Project Overview
Create a fun, interactive community website for REKT, focusing on the ultimate memecoin experience where getting REKT is celebrated as part of the crypto trading journey. The website will showcase the community spirit, trading adventures, and provide an engaging experience for traders and newcomers.

## Core Requirements
- **Community Name**: REKT (The Ultimate Memecoin Experience)
- **Theme**: REKT-themed color scheme with fun, trading-focused aesthetics
- **Technology**: HTML/CSS/JavaScript (responsive design)
- **Assets**: REKT images provided (logo, banner, promotional images)
- **Target Audience**: Crypto traders, memecoin enthusiasts, people who embrace the trading journey

## Key Features to Include
1. **Hero Section**: Welcoming introduction to the REKT experience
2. **About Section**: Community story, mission, and trading values
3. **Features**: What makes REKT special and fun
4. **Community**: Social links and trading engagement opportunities
5. **Roadmap**: Community milestones and future trading adventures
6. **How to Join**: Easy steps to become part of the REKT family
7. **FAQ Section**: Common questions about the community and trading
8. **Footer**: Community links and contact information

## Design Principles
- **Visual Appeal**: Fun and welcoming for traders and community members
- **Mobile-First**: Responsive design that works on all devices
- **Performance**: Fast loading times with optimized images
- **Accessibility**: Proper semantic HTML and color contrast
- **Trading Focus**: Emphasis on trading adventures, getting REKT, and community spirit

## Success Criteria
- Welcoming appearance that makes people want to join the trading community
- Clear communication of REKT values and trading aspects
- Easy navigation and engaging user interface
- Mobile-responsive design
- Fast loading times
- Integration of REKT visual assets
- Emphasis on trading spirit and getting REKT as part of the journey

## Timeline
Single-phase development focusing on creating a complete, engaging community website ready for deployment.
